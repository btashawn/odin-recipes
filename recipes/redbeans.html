Authentic Red Beans & Rice




Red Beans and Rice is a Louisiana Creole dish made with kidney beans, vegetables (primarily bell pepper, onion and celery), spice blends of thyme, cayenne pepper and bay leaf and meat of your choic but primarily: pork bones, ham or sausage. It history can stem back to the Haitian Revolution and the migration of white and freed people of color from Haiti to New Orleans.



Ingredients:

    1 pound dry kidney beans
    6 cups water
    1 large onion, chopped
    1 green bell pepper, chopped
    1/4 cup of olive oil
    2 stalks of celery, chopped
    2 tbsp minced garlic
    1 tbsp dried parsley
    1 tsp of each: cajun seasoning, dried thyme
    1/2 tsp of each: cayenne pepper and dried sage
    1 pound andouille sausage, sliced
    2 bay leaves
    2 cups long grain white rice
    4 cups of water
